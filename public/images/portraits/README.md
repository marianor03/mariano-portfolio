## Guidelines for a clean crossfade

- Same shoot: consistent lighting, background, camera distance/lens and color grade across all three, so the transition reads as one continuous shot rather than three different photos.
- Portrait orientation, at least **2000×2500px**, sRGB JPG (or WebP).
- Keep the subject centered and similarly framed/scaled in all three so the crossfade + scale/parallax doesn't jump.
- Reasonably compressed (aim under ~500KB each) — they'll be optimized further in Phase 2 once real dimensions are known.
